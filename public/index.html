📝 How to Use
Enter a numeric amount using the keypad and tap INPUT to log it. The Total Amount shows your raw running sum. The condition rate (▼/▲) adjusts the Real Amount display.
📊 Three Totals
Total Amount — raw sum of everything you inputted.

Total Real Amount — total ÷ condition rate (e.g. if you entered 90 and condition is 0.90, the real value is 100).

Amount Daily Deal — total × 2.
🎛️ Condition Rate
Tap ▼ or ▲ to adjust the condition rate in 0.01 steps. Hold the button to change rapidly. Use arrow keys on desktop.
⚡ Quick Input
Tap the Quick chips (1, 10, 50, 100, 500) to fill the input instantly, then press INPUT to confirm.
💾 Data Storage
All data is saved locally on your device. Nothing leaves your phone. Data persists between sessions automatically.
📲 Install as App
On Android: tap ⋮ menu → Add to Home Screen. On iPhone: tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Works fully offline once installed.
